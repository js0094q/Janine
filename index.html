HSHH Program

"Happy Spouse, Happy House"

Building relationships that cultivate joy, connection and intimacy—one breath, one step, one day at a time.

We often focus on curating the "perfect" wedding day, invitation, living space or social media post with/about our partner. Yet, rarely do we consider the skills we need to create a life together that is grounded in connection, communication and intention. My goal is to provide a space for you to collaboratively build the foundation for a lasting partnership. Begin your journey with relational alignment so you can flourish individually and together.

Request more info

Who can benefit?

This program is for any partnership desiring to enhance their connection. Whether you are looking for a non-denominational pre-marital program or simply to expand your connection, this program is for you!

The Skills

The first 6 sessions focus on skill building—expanding introspection, understanding attachment, engaging in healthy communication and enhancing connection. The result is more rewarding conversations, greater intimacy and the ability to effectively navigate inevitable conflicts.

The Lens

Sessions 7-12 focus on understanding the lens through which each partner sees the world, as well as exploring what has shaped each partner's view of relationships. This expanded view allows for fewer misperceptions and miscommunications which can lead to disagreements and disconnection.

Mindful Movement

Yoga, Movement, & Co-Regulation

Personalized sessions for children, adolescents, adults, families, and groups.

Mindful movement provides an opportunity to explore mindfulness and movement strategies designed to increase calm, maintain focus and energize the brain and body. Each session is designed to meet the unique needs of the client. Incorporating yoga, movement, mindfulness and the principles of educational kinesiology yield powerful sessions which expand participants' ability to move through life's challenging moments.

What it can include

  • Family co-regulation strategies through movement-based activities and games
  • Specialized sessions for youth athletes targeting strength, mobility, and flexibility
  • Yoga-based mindfulness adapted for schools (students or staff) and corporate groups
  • Individual or small-group work that complements talk therapy
